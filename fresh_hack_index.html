Keep Your Herbs Fresh

We have a tendency to think that anything fresh should be kept in the fridge. Which is true for a lot of vegetables but not often for herbs. Most herbs will keep best in a little water and getting a little sunlight. Vegetables are the fruit of a plant whereas herbs are just a cutting of the plant. Parsley, rosemary, thyme, bay even coriander will keep better in water. I have had rosemary that's lasted for months and started to sprout roots after a while.
